ilaneer payasam - what is that?

the tasty and delicious ilaneer payasam which can be consumed as a dessert after lunch or as a tasty snack.

either way it is gonna be a treat for your taste buds

ingredients

  • ilaneer
  • milk
  • sugar
  • cashews

steps

  1. boil milk
  2. allow it to cool
  3. add sugar and cashews to it
  4. add ilaneer and mix well
  5. voila, your ilaneer payasam is ready
